Where is rome?
Assistant: Rome is the capital city of Italy, located in the central-western part of the country within the Lazio region. It is situated along the Tiber River, approximately 24 kilometers (15 miles) inland from the Tyrrhenian Sea. As one of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in Europe, it is renowned for its historical landmarks, including the Colosseum, Vatican City, the Roman Forum, and the Pantheon. Rome serves as a major political, cultural, and religious center, housing institutions such as the Italian Parliament and the Vatican.
